Challenge Overview

Join our social media challenge and stay consistent for 55 days by posting a photo getting after your goals each day!

Track your progress, share your success, and motivate others to join in!

Challenge Rules

  • Post a minimum of 1 social media post each day for 55 consecutive days.
  • Use the hashtag #55EZ in each of your posts.
  • Keep a record of your daily posts and progress.
  • Share your success and motivate others to join the challenge on social media.
  • Everone is winner in the #55EZ Challenge

    • Tips & Tricks

    Here are some tips and tricks to help you stay motivated and on track:

    • Find a walking partner or group to help keep you accountable.
    • Set small goals for yourself each week to help you stay motivated.
    • Invest in a comfortable pair of walking shoes and clothing.
    • Listen to music or podcasts while you walk to help pass the time.
